A Living Tradition in Glen Echo

From roadside inn to Irish pub, The Irish Inn at Glen Echo has been welcoming guests for generations.

Our Story

The building that is now The Irish Inn at Glen Echo has long been a gathering place. Over the decades it has served as a family home, a biker bar, and a roadside inn before becoming the restaurant and pub our guests know today.

In 2003, the space was reimagined as an Irish inn—pairing the warmth of a traditional pub with a menu that celebrates both Irish classics and contemporary American cuisine.

Today we continue to honor that history while evolving our food, drink, and hospitality to welcome new generations of guests.

At the Heart of Glen Echo

Steps away from Glen Echo Park and the Potomac River, the Inn has become a favorite stop for neighbors, hikers, artists, and visitors exploring the C&O Canal.

Whether you join us for a pint after a walk, Sunday brunch with family, or an evening of live music, our goal is to make every visit feel like coming home.
