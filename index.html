About the Inn

A Historic Gathering Place in Glen Echo

From its early days as a family home and roadside inn to its rebirth as The Irish Inn in 2003, our building has welcomed travelers, neighbors, and artists for generations.

Today, we continue that tradition with thoughtful food, carefully curated drinks, and a welcoming atmosphere inspired by classic Irish pubs.
