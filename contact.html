How to Find Us

The Irish Inn is located beside Glen Echo Park, just off MacArthur Boulevard.

  • Most guests park in the Glen Echo Park lot on Oxford Road.
  • From the lot, follow signs toward the Inn—it’s a short walk.
  • Limited street parking is also available nearby.
